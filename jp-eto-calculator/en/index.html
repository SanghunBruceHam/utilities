Check Your Japanese Zodiac

How to Use:
1. Enter your birthdate (calendar or select boxes).
2. Click the "Convert" button or press Enter.
3. Your zodiac sign, top trending signs, and today's fortune will appear.
* Note: If born before February 4 (Risshun: the first day of spring in the traditional Japanese calendar), the previous year's zodiac is applied.
